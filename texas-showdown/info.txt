3-6 players

original game: Strife
reprinted as Texas Showdown
later reprinted as Seas of Strife

rules differences:
  texas-showdown:
    - if taking with highest card of the suit you can choose who leads the next trick
    - if playing with 3 players, remove the black suit (0-10) and the grey suit (71-74). Player with the 11 starts.
  strife rules:
    - if the highest card of a suit was played, that suit does not count to decide who takes that trick, it is "zeroed".
      - if all suits played in a trick were "zeroed", all the cards played count as a single suit and the highest card wins.
    - if playing wiht 3 players, remove the 3 lowest tricks. Player with the 31 starts
